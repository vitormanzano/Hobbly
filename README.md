▎ Hobbly is a community platform designed for hobby enthusiasts of all levels.
   
  ▎ The project stems from the difficulty of connecting people with shared      
  interests and centralizing knowledge about specific activities — whether 
  questions, tips, achievements, or experiences.

  ▎ Anyone can participate: whether someone is just starting a hobby, has been  
  practicing for years and wants to teach, or simply wants to share their 
  progress. Content is organized through a voting system, where the best        
  contributions gain more visibility within the community.
